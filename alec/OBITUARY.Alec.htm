OBITUARY

 

ALEC STEVEN ROSS

Born February 14, 2002

Died September 7, 2004

 

 

Alec died on September 7, 2004, after choking on a piece of string cheese that he was eating.  Alec is survived by his parents, Gordon and Julia Ross, and his younger brother,  Liam Peter.  Alec’s aunts and uncles: Elizabeth and Mike Metz; Curt and Mary Sager; Ken and Anne Ross-Raymond; Janet and Brian Moore; Hugh Ross and Nancy Ross; and his paternal grandparents: Hugh and Anne Ross; join the family in mourning
Alec’s passing.

 

Alec will be remembered as a best buddy to his little brother.  He was a friendly joyful, playful soul and a healthy active fun-loving kid who brought joy to all who loved him.  In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made to the Alec Steven Ross Memorial Fund c/o First Parish Church in Bedford, 75 The Great Road, Bedford, MA 01730.  Funds will be used to promote public awareness of child choking risks.

 

Family and friends are invited to calling Hours on Thursday, September 9th at the Bedford Unitarian Universalist Church, On the Common 75 The Great Road, Bedford, Massachusetts from 6:00 to 8:00 p.m.  781-275-4086

 

A Memorial Service will be held on Friday, September 10th at the Bedford Unitarian Universalist Church at 11:00 a.m.
