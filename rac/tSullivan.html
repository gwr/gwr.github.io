Ross & Company’s associates are all exceptional consultants with a vast array of skills and experiences in the field of organizational learning and change management. Our network of associates has worked together on projects for close to twenty years. All of our consultants have worked for both public and private sector organizations and have been involved in projects outside of the US.

Thomas Sullivan

Thomas Sullivan has spent 15 years as a consultant, facilitator and lecturer on designing and implementing personal and organization change efforts in distinct settings across the globe. In addition to his work as a management consultant he has been invited to lecture on a variety of topics, including: Coaching, Leadership and Executive Teams, Systems Thinking, Building High Performance Teams, and Innovation and Learning. Mr. Sullivan’s approach is to work with clients’ strengths to create the lasting change they want to bring about while building the capability of the client organization to negotiate the demands of our changing world. Mr. Sullivan’s work includes coaching leadership at the individual and team levels to have breakthrough results in performance in the short-to-medium time frame.

Mr. Sullivan is bilingual (Spanish and English) and works with equal ease in Spanish and English, with clients in Latin America, Spain and the United States. Two recent engagements have involved working in different countries and in English and Spanish. One involved the design and ongoing delivery of a large-scale leadership development program for a multinational company. The second was the implementation of a new organizational structure for the Research and Technical Services arm of a major petrochemical company. This work included coaching the management team and building change and teaming competencies in the staff.

Mr. Sullivan’s work is guided by his philosophy of collaborating with clients to discover and utilize existing internal resources to effect change necessary in their organizations. He is the recipient of several awards in recognition of excellence in delivering value to his clients.

Mr. Sullivan has held senior consulting positions with Arthur D. Little and Innovation Associates. Prior to that, he held a management position with an international training firm, where he managed sales and operations as well as developed and conducted training programs focusing on personal transformation and effectiveness. He has a BA degree from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst.
