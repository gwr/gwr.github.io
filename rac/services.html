Training

Our workshops are designed to help participants increase their effectiveness in producing results through the use of organizational learning and change management methodologies and principles.

Organizational learning is the ability to continually and consistently expand the capacity to create the future. To support this in happening, organizations need skill and proficiency in the following areas:

  • Aspiration – having a clear understanding of what we, individually, and collectively want to create
  • Collaboration – having the ability to work together and to have productive conversations.
  • Understanding complexity and change – being able to objectively examine the natural forces at play and appreciate how they impact our ability to produce results.
We offer a variety of workshops that are designed to develop capacity in these areas. We custom design our workshops to fit the particular needs of our clients. We currently offer:
  • Application of Learning Organization Disciplines
  • Effective Communication
  • Leadership Development
  • Meeting Management & Facilitation Skills
  • Personal Mastery
  • Systems Thinking
  • Team Development
  • Vision Based Strategic Planning

Our approach in teaching our workshops is highly interactive and experiential with limited reliance on lecture. Our standard teaching approach is to introduce a particular framework or tool, have participants practice new skills, then apply those skills to a current work challenge to develop powerful new insights and strategies. A key component is the active and deliberate use of reflection and group inquiry about the practice and application of the theory, methodology, and/or tools and the group’s collective learning.

Facilitation

We have two primary goals whenever we facilitate for our clients.  The goals are to help our clients:

  • Achieve their stated objectives of the session we are facilitating
  • Develop their own facilitation, meeting management and collaboration skills.
Typical facilitation engagements include:
  • Strategic Planning Sessions
  • Kick-off Meetings for New Teams/Projects
  • Off-site Retreats
  • After-Action-Reviews

Consulting & Change Management

Through a combination of workshops, issue-targeted coaching, facilitation and consulting, we can provide powerful team and organizational improvement interventions to help our clients achieve their critical business results. Typical consulting projects include:

  • Addressing chronic or system problems
  • Planning to implement new program initiatives
  • Building internal consulting capacity
  • Embarking on a large scale change project
