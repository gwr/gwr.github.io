Julia Ross

Julia Ross is a Principal in Ross & Company, a firm specializing in organizational change and learning that she founded in 1998. Ms. Ross is an experienced organizational development consultant who works with individuals, teams and organizations to achieve their critical business results and to develop their capacity to learn.

The focus of Ms. Ross’s work is to help her clients (both individuals and teams) increase their effectiveness in producing results. She does this through using organizational learning methodologies in her training, coaching, and consulting work as well as through her facilitation.

Ms. Ross’s training programs cover a broad range of topics that include: leadership, organizational learning, change management, facilitation, consulting skills, coaching, team development and communication. In addition to leading these programs, she also trains internal consultants to lead the programs within their own organizations.

The setting for much of Ms. Ross’s facilitation work includes offsite retreats, kick-off meetings for new teams/projects, and strategic planning sessions. Julia’s facilitation methodology is one where her focus is twofold: to help her clients achieve the specified meeting results as well as develop their own facilitation, meeting management, and collaboration skills.

Prior to founding Ross & Company, Ms. Ross worked 13 years for Innovation Associates, Inc. She held various management positions prior to joining their consulting practice. Innovation Associates was co-founded by Peter Senge, the author of the best selling book, The Fifth Discipline.

Ms. Ross holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Management from the University of Vermont. She lives in the Boston area with her husband and two children.
