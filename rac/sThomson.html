Suzanne Thomson

Sue Thomson is a Senior Partner with Ross & Company and also the founder and Managing Director of Trinity Partners Consulting of Providence, Rhode Island. Her clients are leaders of business, government and nonprofit organizations interested in achieving successful, long-lasting results by applying the disciplines of organizational learning, commitment based change and skillful teamwork. Recently, she has focused on not for profit and governmental organizations, and on bringing together people and groups with diverse viewpoints on critical issues to find common ground, agree upon top priorities and craft policy agendas they can mutually support.

A consultant to management for 20+ years, on her own and with Innovation Associates, Arthur D. Little, and Rath & Strong, Ms. Thomson has also managed large system change from the “inside” as a line manager and, later, Vice President for Management & Organization Development with Security Pacific Bank, and as Director of Human Resource Planning and Development with Analog Devices, Inc. Most recently, she served as the Deputy Commissioner of Public Health for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

Ms. Thomson is a contributor to The Fifth Discipline Fieldbook and The Dance of Change and is author of various articles on change, leadership, mergers and process improvement. She holds an MBA, with an emphasis in organization behavior, from the University of Southern California. She completed her undergraduate work at the University of Wisconsin, Madison.

When not working with clients you are likely to find her in dusty records vaults poring over very old documents in search of the trail left behind by her family’s ancestors and wishing she could find the link back to Europe so she can justify a nice long vacation there.
